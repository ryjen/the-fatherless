## Style Prompt

A cinematic moral epic drawn from ancient Aurelia: dusk skyline, torchlit stone, linen, bronze, oxblood, candle smoke, and solemn public ritual. The preview should feel like an artifact from a lost empire being brought into motion, with restrained typography, weathered textures, and transitions that behave like shutters, veils, and light passing over carved stone.

## Colors

- `#17100d` - soot-black background
- `#efe0c2` - warm linen foreground
- `#c88b3f` - deep gold accent
- `#8f2f24` - oxblood tension accent
- `#4d5f63` - oxidized stone secondary

## Typography

- Display: `Fraunces`, high weight, used for names and moral declarations.
- Support: `IBM Plex Sans`, light and medium weights, used for narration and labels.
- Data and scene markers: `Azeret Mono`, tabular where numbers appear.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon, purple-blue gradients, or generic tech styling.
- Do not make repeated card grids; scenes should feel composed like cinema stills.
- Do not use pure black or pure white; tint neutrals toward soot and linen.
- Do not center every element with equal weight; keep a visible editorial hierarchy.
- Do not use modern UI motifs that break the ancient historical tone.
