# **Scene 2 – The Sky Burns**

**Location:** Winter solstice night
**Time:** Night

---

## **EXT. TUNDRA CAMP – NIGHT**

The sky erupts in a curtain of red light. Auroras ripple like fire across ice.
People fall to their knees. The fire crackles, reflected in terrified eyes.

**ELDER (whispering):**
"The Sky-Fire wakes."

A wave of light pours over the camp, turning snow to blood.

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Divine terror**: The heavens feel alive and angry.
* **A sign in flame**: The world changes in a single night.
* **Fear as ritual**: The people respond with awe.
