# **Scene 12 – The First Teachings**

**Location:** New settlement
**Time:** Evening

---

## **EXT. SETTLEMENT FIRE – EVENING**

Atuun teaches children to shape tools and share food.
Hunters and elders gather as he speaks quietly.

**ATUUN:**
"No one survives alone. The fire is for all."

The people repeat the words, a new covenant forming.

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Unity becomes law**: Mercy shapes a new society.
* **Civilization begins**: Shared rules replace rivalry.
* **Fire as community**: Warmth becomes collective.
