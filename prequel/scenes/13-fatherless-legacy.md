# **Scene 13 – The Fatherless Legacy**

**Location:** Generational montage
**Time:** Centuries later

---

## **MONTAGE – GENERATIONS**

Cave art shows a child beneath a burning sky.
Fires pass from hand to hand. New lands bloom.
Atuun’s name is spoken in ritual and song.

**ELDER (V.O.):**
"He was born of fire, not of kings."

The image fades into embers.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Mythic inheritance**: Survival becomes story.
* **The first fatherless leader**: Destiny without lineage echoes forward.
* **Cycle begins**: Atuun’s legacy foreshadows Sael.
