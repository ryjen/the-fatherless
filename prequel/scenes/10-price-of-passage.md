# **Scene 10 – The Price of Passage**

**Location:** River gorge
**Time:** Day

---

## **EXT. RIVER GORGE – DAY**

The group builds a rope bridge. Ice melts underfoot.
An elder slips, clinging to the fire bowl. Atuun grabs his arm.

**ELDER (soft):**
"Carry it."

The elder lets go, falling into the river. Atuun holds the fire, shaking.

---

## **EXT. RIVERBANK – DAY**

The group mourns in silence. Atuun places a stone marker by the water.

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Sacrifice for the future**: Lives are traded for survival.
* **Fire as inheritance**: The ember is passed through loss.
* **Leadership burden**: Atuun carries grief forward.
