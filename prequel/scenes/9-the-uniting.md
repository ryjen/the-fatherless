# **Scene 9 – The Uniting**

**Location:** Desertifying plains
**Time:** Day

---

## **EXT. DRY PLAINS – DAY**

Two rival tribes clash over a water source. Atuun steps between them, arms open.
He kneels and sets his fire on the ground.

**ATUUN:**
"We were born of warmth. We must return to it together."

A rival leader lowers his spear. Others follow.

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Unity as survival**: Joining forces ends violence.
* **Mercy as leadership**: Atuun offers trust, not conquest.
* **The first people**: A proto-civilization begins.
