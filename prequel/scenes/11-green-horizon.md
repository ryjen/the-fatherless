# **Scene 11 – The Green Horizon**

**Location:** African savanna
**Time:** Day

---

## **EXT. SAVANNA – DAY**

Tall grass rolls under a warm wind. Herds move across open plains.
The travelers fall to their knees, stunned by the abundance.

**HUNTER (tears):**
"The world is alive."

Atuun lifts the fire toward the sun.

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Home regained**: Warmth returns to the people.
* **Forgiveness of the earth**: The land welcomes them.
* **Promise fulfilled**: The journey’s faith is rewarded.
