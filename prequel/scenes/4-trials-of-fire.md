# **Scene 4 – Trials of Fire**

**Location:** Tundra and caves
**Time:** Years later

---

## **EXT. TUNDRA RIDGE – DAY**

A teenage Atuun tracks a wounded deer through drifting snow.
A wolf pack circles. He sets a line of fire with dry grass, driving them back.

---

## **INT. CAVE – NIGHT**

Atuun studies charcoal markings of stars and animal paths. He scratches new lines beside them.
An elder watches, uneasy.

**ELDER:**
"You read the sky like a map."

**ATUUN:**
"It is the only map we have."

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Knowledge as survival**: Observation becomes guidance.
* **Courage without cruelty**: Fire protects without killing.
* **The mind awakens**: Atuun becomes a compass.
