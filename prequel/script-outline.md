# **Title:** *The Fatherless: Age of Embers*

**Genre:** Prehistoric Survival / Mythic Epic

**Main Story:** [The Fatherless](../script-outline.md)

**Sequel:** [The Fatherless II: Neurion](../sequel/)

---

## **ACT I – The Sky Burns**

---

### **Scene 1 – The Frozen World**

**Scene file:** [scenes/1-frozen-world.md](scenes/1-frozen-world.md)

**Location:** Ice-age France – Tundra camps

* Humans struggle against predators and hunger; fire is guarded like a living soul.
* Tribes clash over scarce hunting grounds; the north grows harsher each season.

**Visuals:** White plains, wind-carved ice, a single fire ringed by bodies.  
**Symbolism:** Survival as faith; fire as identity.

---

### **Scene 2 – The Sky Burns**

**Scene file:** [scenes/2-sky-burns.md](scenes/2-sky-burns.md)

**Location:** Winter solstice night

* A massive solar flare ignites the sky; auroras bleed across the ice.
* The people believe the Sky-Fire god has awakened in wrath.

**Visuals:** Blood-red auroras, terrified faces lit by firelight.  
**Symbolism:** Divine terror; a world reset by flame.

---

### **Scene 3 – Atuun’s Birth**

**Scene file:** [scenes/3-atuun-birth.md](scenes/3-atuun-birth.md)

**Location:** Hidden cavern beneath ice cliffs

* A child is born at the height of the blaze; elders declare him chosen.
* He is named **Atuun**, the one caught between fire and night.

**Dialogue:**  
**Elder:** “A leader has been ignited from the fury of the heavens.”  
**Visuals:** Cavern shadows, newborn breath in the cold.  
**Symbolism:** Destiny without lineage; the first fatherless spark.

---

### **Scene 4 – Trials of Fire**

**Scene file:** [scenes/4-trials-of-fire.md](scenes/4-trials-of-fire.md)

**Location:** Tundra and caves

* Atuun survives storms and predators beyond his years.
* He studies bone patterns, stars, and cave art; his mind is the tribe’s new compass.

**Visuals:** A boy under starlight, scratches on stone, the glow of embers.  
**Symbolism:** Knowledge as survival; intuition as gift.

## **ACT II – The Prophecy of Return**

---

### **Scene 5 – The Question of the South**

**Scene file:** [scenes/5-question-of-south.md](scenes/5-question-of-south.md)

**Location:** Elders’ circle

* Atuun asks why they remain in the frozen north.
* Elders speak of Africa, a forgotten warmth beyond memory.

**Dialogue:**  
**Atuun:** “We were born of warmth. Why do we die in ice?”  
**Visuals:** Elder hands over embers; charcoal maps on stone.  
**Symbolism:** Memory as prophecy; warmth as home.

---

### **Scene 6 – The Council Divided**

**Scene file:** [scenes/6-council-divided.md](scenes/6-council-divided.md)

**Location:** Clan gathering

* Hunters fear the journey; others cling to tradition.
* Atuun argues the Sky-Fire was not wrath, but instruction.

**Visuals:** Faces split by firelight, wind clawing at hides.  
**Symbolism:** Tradition versus revelation.

---

### **Scene 7 – The First Departure**

**Scene file:** [scenes/7-first-departure.md](scenes/7-first-departure.md)

**Location:** Edge of the ice plain

* Atuun leads a small band south; others remain behind.
* The journey begins with uncertain faith and dwindling supplies.

**Visuals:** Footprints in snow, a fire carried in a clay bowl.  
**Symbolism:** Threshold crossing; hope as burden.

---

## **ACT III – The Long Migration**

---

### **Scene 8 – The Ice Crossing**

**Scene file:** [scenes/8-ice-crossing.md](scenes/8-ice-crossing.md)

**Location:** Alpine passes

* Predators stalk the group; a blizzard nearly extinguishes their fire.
* Atuun rescues the flame, earning deeper trust.

**Visuals:** Whiteout storm, a single ember saved in cupped hands.  
**Symbolism:** The fire survives; so do the people.

---

### **Scene 9 – The Uniting**

**Scene file:** [scenes/9-the-uniting.md](scenes/9-the-uniting.md)

**Location:** Desertifying plains

* Atuun encounters rival tribes; violence threatens to erupt.
* He offers shared fire and new hunting methods; the tribes join.

**Dialogue:**  
**Atuun:** “We were born of warmth. We must return to it—together.”  
**Visuals:** Fires merging, spears lowered.  
**Symbolism:** Unity as survival; leadership by mercy.

---

### **Scene 10 – The Price of Passage**

**Scene file:** [scenes/10-price-of-passage.md](scenes/10-price-of-passage.md)

**Location:** River gorge

* A crossing claims lives; a mentor dies protecting the fire.
* Atuun absorbs grief and carries the burden forward.

**Visuals:** Bodies in rushing water, a torch lifted into dawn.  
**Symbolism:** Sacrifice for the future; fire as inheritance.

## **ACT IV – The Return to Warmth**

---

### **Scene 11 – The Green Horizon**

**Scene file:** [scenes/11-green-horizon.md](scenes/11-green-horizon.md)

**Location:** African savanna

* The travelers reach fertile lands; rains return, game is abundant.
* The people weep, believing the Sky-Fire has forgiven them.

**Visuals:** Tall grass moving like waves, faces bathed in sun.  
**Symbolism:** Home regained; warmth as promise.

---

### **Scene 12 – The First Teachings**

**Scene file:** [scenes/12-first-teachings.md](scenes/12-first-teachings.md)

**Location:** New settlement

* Atuun teaches unity, migration, and the sanctity of life.
* The tribe becomes the earliest proto-civilization.

**Visuals:** Shared fires, children learning to shape tools.  
**Symbolism:** Civilization born from mercy.

---

### **Scene 13 – The Fatherless Legacy**

**Scene file:** [scenes/13-fatherless-legacy.md](scenes/13-fatherless-legacy.md)

**Location:** Generational montage

* Atuun’s story becomes myth; his name echoes across ages.

**Symbolism:** Survival becomes myth; the cycle begins.
