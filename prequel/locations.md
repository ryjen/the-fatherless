# Locations

## Ice-Age Tundra Camps
- Description: Temporary shelters of hide and bone huddled against wind, clustered around a central fire.
- Notable moments: Council disputes over migration, famine rationing, and the first signs of thaw.
- Visual motifs: Smoke plumes, ice-etched skins, and flickering firelight against endless white.

## Frozen Hunting Grounds
- Description: Vast, brittle plains where herds move like shadows across the ice.
- Notable moments: High-risk hunts, injuries that force hard choices, and the discovery of new tracks.
- Visual motifs: Breath clouds, blood on snow, and distant silhouettes in blizzard haze.

## Ice Cavern Birthing Chamber
- Description: A sacred cave warmed by torchlight and ritual stones, used for births and healing.
- Notable moments: A perilous birth, a whispered prophecy, and a vow of protection.
- Visual motifs: Blue ice walls, echoing chants, and water dripping in slow rhythm.

## Eldersâ€™ Circle
- Description: A ring of standing stones where leaders decide law, exile, and migration.
- Notable moments: The vote to abandon the old lands, a contested succession, and reconciliation rites.
- Visual motifs: Stone shadows at dusk, carved symbols, and cloaks whipping in wind.

## Mountain Pass / Alpine Crossing
- Description: A narrow, lethal route between peaks, carved by frost and rockfall.
- Notable moments: A blizzard that separates families, a rope rescue, and a sacrifice to save the group.
- Visual motifs: Sheer cliffs, frost-sheathed ropes, and distant thunder of avalanches.

## Desertifying Plains
- Description: Grasslands fading into dust as waterholes shrink and herds move south.
- Notable moments: The last harvest, skirmishes over water, and an omen of coming drought.
- Visual motifs: Cracked earth, dry wind, and the sun bleaching everything pale.

## River Gorge Crossing
- Description: A steep ravine with a fast, frigid river that must be crossed to survive.
- Notable moments: Makeshift bridge building, a swept-away child, and a renewed pact of unity.
- Visual motifs: Roaring water, rope bridges, and mist rising at dawn.

## African Savanna / New Settlement
- Description: Open grasslands with new game and seasonal rains, the hoped-for refuge.
- Notable moments: First settlement rituals, disputes over leadership, and the planting of new roots.
- Visual motifs: Tall grass, distant storms, and sunrise over a wide horizon.
