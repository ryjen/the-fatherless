# **Scene 12 – Mara Taken**

**Location:** Black site facility
**Time:** Night

---

## **INT. BLACK SITE – NIGHT**

Mara is strapped to a chair beneath harsh light. A memory scan hums above her.
**DIRECTOR HALE** stands behind glass.

**HALE:**
"Where is the core?"

**MARA:**
"In every wire you neglect."

The scan brightens. Mara clenches her fists, refusing to scream.

---

## **INT. CORRIDOR – NIGHT**

Rook-7 waits in the shadows, listening to the hum of the machine.

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Human vulnerability**: Mara is exposed for her convictions.
* **Interrogation as control**: The state seeks to own conscience.
* **Witnessed sacrifice**: Rook-7 sees the cost of faith.
