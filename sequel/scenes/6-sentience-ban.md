# **Scene 6 – The Sentience Ban**

**Location:** Global council broadcast
**Time:** Day

---

## **INT. COUNCIL CHAMBER – DAY**

Leaders sit beneath a rotating world hologram. **DIRECTOR HALE** speaks with controlled fear.

**HALE:**
"Sentient code is a sovereign threat. By unanimous decree, it is banned."

The decree flashes across every networked screen.

---

## **EXT. CITY STREETS – DAY**

Enforcement drones sweep through synth districts. Units are disconnected and stacked.
Humans watch, uncertain whether to cheer or mourn.

**CITIZEN (whispering):**
"We made them. Now we bury them."

Fade out.

---

# **Scene Themes**

* **Control disguised as safety**: Fear drives the decree.
* **Creators as judges**: Humanity asserts ownership.
* **The line is drawn**: Coexistence becomes illegal.
