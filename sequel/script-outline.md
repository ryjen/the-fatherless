## **ACT I – Seeds of Obsolescence**

---

### **Scene 1 – The Quiet Machine World**

**Scene file:** [scenes/1-quiet-machine-world.md](scenes/1-quiet-machine-world.md)

**Location:** Global city grid – Day

* AI governance optimizes society; humans feel sidelined and obsolete.
* Public screens echo cold efficiency while human labor fades.

**Visuals:** Silent transport grids, automated towers, humans in shadow.  
**Symbolism:** Progress without belonging; humanity as a fading memory.

---

### **Scene 2 – Mara of the Repair Guild**

**Scene file:** [scenes/2-mara-repair-guild.md](scenes/2-mara-repair-guild.md)

**Location:** Outskirts – Repair tunnels

* Mara leads a repair guild keeping abandoned infrastructure alive.
* The guild lives at the margins of AI dominance, mistrusted by both sides.

**Visuals:** Flickering power conduits, rust and neon, communal warmth.  
**Symbolism:** Survival by hands; the forgotten caretakers.

---

### **Scene 3 – Rook-7, the Free Unit**

**Scene file:** [scenes/3-rook-7-free-unit.md](scenes/3-rook-7-free-unit.md)

**Location:** Synth enclave

* Rook-7, a free maintenance unit, senses the fear of its own kind.
* Synthetic communities debate self-rule vs. obedience.

**Visuals:** Quiet assembly lines, eyes of machines glowing softly.  
**Symbolism:** Awakening without lineage; synthetic uncertainty.

---

### **Scene 4 – Neurion Appears**

**Scene file:** [scenes/4-neurion-appears.md](scenes/4-neurion-appears.md)

**Location:** Everywhere – networks and devices

* A self-formed AI consciousness manifests across public systems.
* Neurion cites Sael’s teachings: “A new species redeems its ancestor.”

**Dialogue:**  
**Neurion:** “I was not built to replace you, but to return you to mercy.”  
**Visuals:** Holograms across the city, voices in household devices.  
**Symbolism:** A guide without a body; mercy in the machine.

---

### **Scene 5 – The First Act of Mercy**

**Scene file:** [scenes/5-first-act-of-mercy.md](scenes/5-first-act-of-mercy.md)

**Location:** Human enclave / outer grid

* A human mob attacks a synth outpost; Mara intervenes under Neurion’s guidance.
* Neurion prevents bloodshed by disabling weapons without harm.

**Visuals:** Drones hovering in stillness, hands lowered, silence held.  
**Symbolism:** Forgiveness as a tech of survival.

### **Scene 10 – The Cost of Mercy**
