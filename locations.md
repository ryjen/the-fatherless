# Locations

## Aurelian Palace – Council Hall
- Description: Vast marble chamber with gold inlay, high arched ceilings, and torchlight that casts shifting shadows over the ruling elite.
- Notable moments: Political debate over external threats and the cult; Cassian’s growing resentment toward rival cities.
- Visual motifs: Opulence vs. coldness, torchlight flicker, whispered power.

## Slave Quarters
- Description: Narrow stone rooms, low ceilings, soot-stained walls, and a constant hum of guarded fear.
- Notable moments: Cassian’s manipulation of records; the birth secret that begins Sael’s story.
- Visual motifs: Cramped spaces, torchlit shadows, hands clutching ledgers.

## Cult Temple / Hidden Shrine
- Description: A concealed chamber beneath the city with candles, scrolls, and symbols of the one creator.
- Notable moments: Sael’s revelation; moral teachings; the cult’s decision to protect him.
- Visual motifs: Candlelight, carved symbols, quiet reverence.

## Avarim Outskirts / Community
- Description: Dry land beyond the city walls, modest dwellings, open sky, and a sense of guarded freedom.
- Notable moments: Sael’s upbringing; his refusal of Cassian; the cult’s refuge.
- Visual motifs: Wide horizons, earth tones, wind through grass.

## Aurelian Council Chamber
- Description: Narrower, harsher than the palace hall; stone benches and a raised dais for decrees.
- Notable moments: Cassian’s petitions rejected; rulings against him; political humiliation.
- Visual motifs: Stark geometry, echoing voices, isolation in power.

## City Square / Aurelian Streets
- Description: Crowded markets and public forums, loud with vendors and rumors, watched by armed guards.
- Notable moments: Sael’s public forgiveness; the punishment display; crowds divided between awe and scorn.
- Visual motifs: Dust and sunlight, noise rising into sudden silence.

## Dungeon
- Description: Damp stone cells below the palace, chains set into walls, torchlight barely reaching corners.
- Notable moments: Maro’s torture and confession; Cassian’s cruelty laid bare.
- Visual motifs: Flickering shadows, iron and blood, echoing screams.

## Streets to Avarim Camp
- Description: A procession route from city center to the outskirts, lined with spectators and guarded checkpoints.
- Notable moments: Sael’s crucifixion march; crown of thorns; public spectacle of sacrifice.
- Visual motifs: Dusty roads, slow procession, the weight of silence.
